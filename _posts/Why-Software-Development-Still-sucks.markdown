The software industry has now for at least 20 years the tools at hand it needs to build great software with high quality. The book "Accelerate" even provides empirical proof that these things work.

The problem is the management that is in charge of steering the culture of the company. Tech tech staff of a company is not able to change the culture of the company without the buy-in from the management.

How to fix this is something that I don't have a good answer for and I doubt other people have it either. At least my research has not shown that there is any "silver bullet" to address this problem.
