Domain-Driven Design (DDD) techniques and practices.

## Techniques

- [Event Storming](/ddd/event-storming/) – A versatile technique that leverages domain events to explore and model complex business processes.
- [Domain Storytelling](/ddd/domain-storytelling/) – A collaborative modeling technique to capture and understand business processes through visual stories.
